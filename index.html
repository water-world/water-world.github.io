About

Welcome to Water World! The fun experience where you will learn to water paint, as well as learn the history of water painting. You will be able to see the many different works of some artists as well as have some tutorial videos to learn the different strokes and styles to water paintings.


Water color first began in ancient Chinese Dynasties, soon after that it was adopted heavily from Europe, which was heavily used in the Renaiassance era. Coming to modern times, water color painting is used as a form of expression for many artists, ranging from beginners to the more advanced painters.
